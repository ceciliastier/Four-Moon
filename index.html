About Us

Four Moon Productions is a full-service audio/visual production company. From set design and content creation to streaming and everything in between, Four Moon is a one-stop solution for live corporate events. Four Moon is a completely mobile production company, servicing all corners of the globe.

Why Four Moon? Four Moon was built on a foundation of integrity, providing a high level of service with a strategic and very reasonable pricing model. Quite simply, we care. We care about the service we provide and exceeding our client’s expectations on every program we produce.

Four Moon Leadership

Curtis Johnson

Sales Director

Curtis Johnson, a native of Omaha, Nebraska, moved to Las Vegas in 2001 and began his production career. His knowledge of the industry’s inner-workings, decades of experience, and his strong emphasis on customer service led him into the position of Sales Director with Four Moon Productions. He prides himself on helping clients with seamless execution from concept to successful completion.

Brent Sedlacek

Chief Operating Officer

As COO, Brent uses his wealth of knowledge and experience to direct all aspects of execution for Four Moon events. He often works as the technical director for shows and excels in planning and executing events. He has a diverse video background with a full understanding of how to design and engineer large video systems. He is an expert show caller as he spent years in broadcast learning from some of the best directors in the world on many live events. He is well versed in digital drawings, production management, crew supervision, directing shows, and all other aspects of live event production.

Drew Lehmann

Director of Business Development

Drew was actually a client of Four Moon prior to joining the FMP team. Drew brings a unique perspective as he was the content and speaker coordinator for a major real estate conference. He grew up in the world of A/V and previously owned a production company in Louisiana. Additionally, he worked in television and professional sports broadcasting.

Callie Frontzak

Office Manager

Callie joins Four Moon with vast experience in the service industry in which she constantly excels with her positive, outgoing attitude, and ability to put others’ needs first. She handles a variety of tasks for FMP ranging from daily operations to onsite production assistance with an added splash of style and grace!

Malcolm Henderson

Director of Operations

A native of Austin, TX, Malcolm has over ten years of experience in video and live event production. After a number of years filming surgeries, wildfires, live music events, and commercials, Malcolm transitioned to live event production where he has run the gamut from video and graphics operation all the way to video lead and technical director roles. His interpersonal skills complement his technical skills, ensuring that clients experience a world-class relationship with Four Moon Productions.

JT Naughton

Founder and President

A native of Chicago, JT has 20+ years of experience in the production world. JT founded Four Moon after recognizing a real need for customer service in the production industry. JT started the company with a core value of putting people first and as a result, Four Moon has benefited from a loyal and expanding list of customers.
